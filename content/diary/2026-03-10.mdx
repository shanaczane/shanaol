this one stays between me and the page.

there's a particular kind of 3am clarity that only happens when the rest of the world has gone quiet. the thoughts that come then are the ones I don't know how to say out loud.

I spent a long time tonight thinking about the gap between who I am online and who I am when no one's watching. not in a bad way. just... noticing it. the curation. the version of yourself you build in public is still *you*, but it's the you that's been through an editor.

I think I prefer unedited. even when it's messier.

the site is live now. it's a strange thing to put yourself out there like this. even a carefully constructed self is still a self. someone is going to read these words and form an idea of who I am.

I hope the idea is at least a little right.
