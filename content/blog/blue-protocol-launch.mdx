okay so I said I was *just going to try it for an hour*.

it is now 4am.

## first impressions

the character creator alone kept me busy for 45 minutes. i made the most chaotic little gremlin and i have zero regrets. the art style is everything i wanted — anime aesthetics with actual good combat mechanics.

combat feels snappy. the dodge timing takes a bit to get used to but once it clicks it's so satisfying. my class is Blast Archer and i've already fallen off a cliff trying to aim at something above me.

## the world

genuinely beautiful. the zone i started in had this golden hour lighting that made me stop and just... stand there for a moment. games don't usually do that to me in the first hour.

the lore seems deep but i haven't had time to read any of it because i keep getting distracted by side quests and pretty vistas.

## verdict so far

it's good. it's really good. i need to sleep and then play more.

send help (or coffee).
