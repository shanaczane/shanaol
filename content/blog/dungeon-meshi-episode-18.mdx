I was not prepared.

I watched episode 18 last night and I am still thinking about it. This show keeps doing this thing where it lures you in with cute food content and then *stabs you directly in the heart*.

## no spoilers but also mild spoilers

The Falin stuff hit different this episode. Everything that's been building for the past few arcs came to a head and I just sat there with my mouth open for like three minutes after the credits rolled.

Ryoko Kui is a genius. The way she constructed this story — using food and cooking as a lens for everything the characters are going through — is one of the most elegant narrative choices I've seen in manga, and the anime adaptation is doing it full justice.

## the animation

Studio Trigger really said *okay we're going to make this frame perfect* and then did exactly that. The action sequences in this episode were stunning.

## where to go from here

I already know what happens because I read the manga, but watching it unfold is a completely different experience. My heart hurts and I love it.

10/10 please watch Dungeon Meshi.
